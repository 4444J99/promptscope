      Why PromptScope
      
    
      System prompts get written once and forgotten. Six months later they're full of contradictions, dead instructions, anti-patterns the model is silently working around. PromptScope reads your prompt with a model trained to find those issues — fast, cheap, and unsentimental.
      
    
      Built for: AI engineers shipping production prompts • Teams maintaining prompt libraries • Researchers iterating on prompt designs.
      
  
      

  
      
    
      FAQ
      
    
      Is my prompt sent to a third party?
      Analysis runs on Cloudflare Workers AI (Llama 3.3 / Mistral). Prompts are not stored unless you use the share-link feature, which creates an opaque permalink visible only to those you share with. Default: ephemeral, no logging.
      
    
      What anti-patterns does it look for?
      Vague instructions, role contradictions, missing output schemas, persona-before-task ordering, capability over-claims, walls-of-text without structure, missing examples, missing constraints, hidden tool-use assumptions.
      
    
      Why a paid tier?
      The free tier covers light usage. Pro funds the inference compute and pays for ongoing development. Pro users also get the rewrite suggestions, API access, and team features.
